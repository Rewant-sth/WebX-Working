Trip Overview

The Nepal White Water Rafting Adventure combines multiple river systems to provide the ultimate white water experience, flowing from the Himalayan foothills through subtropical valleys to the Terai plains.

Your adventure begins in Kathmandu (1,400 m), where equipment is checked and final preparations made. A scenic drive brings you to Charaudi (600 m), the traditional put-in point for Trishuli River expeditions. This legendary river, flowing from the glaciers of Langtang Himal, provides consistent Grade II-III rapids through terraced landscapes dotted with traditional villages.

The Trishuli River section includes famous rapids like "Ladies Delight," "Upset," and "Surprise", each offering unique challenges and thrills. Between rapids, peaceful stretches allow for wildlife observation, photography, and cultural interactions with riverside communities.

Transitioning to the Seti River system, you experience Nepal's "white river," renowned for its remarkably clear, warm waters flowing through pristine subtropical valleys. The Seti offers technical rapids including "Pinball," "Ram's Horn," and "Washing Machine," each requiring teamwork and precision paddling.

The multi-day format includes:

  • River camping under star-filled skies with traditional Nepali meals

  • Cultural village visits to experience authentic rural life

  • Wildlife observation including river dolphins, kingfishers, and otters

  • Optional extensions to Chitwan National Park for elephant safaris

Safety remains paramount throughout, with certified guides, comprehensive safety briefings, and top-quality equipment ensuring your adventure is both thrilling and secure.


Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Kathmandu at Tribhuvan International Airport

Namaste and welcome to Nepal! Upon arrival at Tribhuvan International Airport, you are greeted by our team and transferred to your hotel in Thamel. The bustling streets, vibrant prayer flags, and adventure energy prepare you for your thrilling rafting expedition. In the evening, enjoy a welcome dinner with traditional Nepali cuisine and a comprehensive briefing about your white water adventure.

🏨 Accommodation: Hotel in Kathmandu

Day 3: Trishuli River Rafting to Kurintar

Your adventure begins with Grade II-III rapids as you navigate the Trishuli River's crystal-clear waters. Thunder through exciting rapids like "Ladies Delight" and "Upset Rapids" while surrounded by terraced hills and subtropical forests. Between the adrenaline rushes, enjoy peaceful stretches where you can spot kingfishers, eagles, and playful river otters. Overnight camping by the riverside under starlit skies.

🍽️ Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
🏨 Accommodation: Resort
🏃‍♂️‍➡️ Activities: Rafting
Day 2: Drive from Kathmandu to Charaudi

After breakfast, embark on a scenic drive west along the Prithvi Highway toward the rafting put-in point. The road winds past terraced hillsides, rushing tributaries, and traditional villages. You arrive at Charaudi, where the mighty Trishuli River beckons. Here, expert guides provide safety briefings, equipment fitting, and paddling instructions before launching into your first rapids.


🍽️ Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
🏨 Accommodation: Resort
🏃‍♂️‍➡️ Activities: Drive
Day 5: Seti River Rafting to Saranghat

Launch into the pristine waters of the Seti River, known for its warm, clear waters and consistent Grade II-III rapids. Navigate through "Pinball Rapids" and "Ram's Horn" while surrounded by dense forests and terraced hillsides. The Seti offers perfect swimming opportunities in calm sections, and you might spot freshwater dolphins in deeper pools. Beach camping with traditional Nepali dinner around a campfire.

🍽️ Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner
🏨 Accommodation: Resort
🏃‍♂️‍➡️ Activities: Rafting
Day 4: Continue to Seti River and Drive to Damauli

After breakfast by the river, drive through scenic countryside to reach the Seti River system. The landscape transforms into lush valleys dotted with traditional Gurung and Magar villages. Upon arrival at Damauli, enjoy lunch while guides prepare equipment for the next phase of your multi-river adventure.

🍽️ Meals: Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner

Day 6: Seti River to Chitwan - Wildlife Extension

Your final day on the Seti includes thrilling morning rapids before reaching the confluence with larger river systems. After lunch by the river, transfer to Chitwan National Park for an optional wildlife extension. If choosing the wildlife package, enjoy elephant safari preparation; otherwise, continue toward Kathmandu with stops at local villages for cultural interactions.

After breakfast, begin the scenic journey back to Kathmandu, traveling through the heart of Nepal's middle hills. Stop at traditional pottery villages and local markets where you can purchase handicrafts.
